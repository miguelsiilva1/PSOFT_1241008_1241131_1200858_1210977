# Glossary

**Terms, Expressions and Acronyms (TEA) must be organized alphabetically.**

| **_TEA_**                          | **_TEA (Portuguese)_**                                                                                | **_Description_**                                                                                                                                                                 |                                       
|:-----------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Aircraft Model**                 | Modelo da Aeronave                                                                                    | Is a physical model of an existing aircraft.                                                                                                                                      |
| **Aircraft Registration Number**   | Número de Registo de uma Aeronave                                                                     | An unique code assigned to a specific aircraft, identical to a car's license plate.                                                                                               |
| **Aircraft Seating Configuration** | Configuração de Assentos de uma Aeronave                                                              | Is the arrangment of an aircraft seats, galleys, lavatories and other features.                                                                                                   |
| **Aircraft Flight Hours**          | Horas de Voo da Aeronave                                                                              | The accumulated total of operational flying hours of a specific aircraft.                                                                                                         |
| **Aircraft Status**                | Estado da Aeronave                                                                                    | It refers to the current status or condition of an aircraft.                                                                                                                      |
| **Airplane Certification**         | Certificação de Aeronaves                                                                             | A term used to characterize the permission, owned by an aircraft, to fly to/from an airport.                                                                                      |
| **Airport Gates**                  | Portão de um Aeroporto                                                                                | An airport gate is an airport terminal that controls access to a passenger aircraft.                                                                                              |
| **Airport Location**               | Localização de um Aeroporto                                                                           | Geographical position per city, country and region on an airport.                                                                                                                 | 
| **Airport Services**               | Serviços de um Aeroporto                                                                              | Airport services refers to the various services provided at an airport.                                                                                                           |
| **Airport Status**                 | Estado de um Aeroporto                                                                                | It refers to the current status or condition of an airport.                                                                                                                       |
| **Airport Terminals**              | Terminal de um Aeroporto                                                                              | An airport terminal is the a main building at the airport where passengers check in, go through security, and board or disembark from an aircraft.                                |
| **Airport Type**                   | Tipo de um Aeroporto                                                                                  | Its the distribution of airport functions.                                                                                                                                        |
| **Assignment**                     | Atribuição de voo                                                                                     | A term used to express the same as "flight".                                                                                                                                      |
| **ATCC**                           | Colaborador de Empresa de Transporte Aéreo                                                            | An acronym used to identify a Air Transport Company Collaborator.                                                                                                                 |
| **Backoffice Operator**            | Operador de _Backoffice_                                                                              | A professional or team responsible for managing the administrative and operational support functions related to aircraft operations.                                              |
| **Checklist**                      | Lista de Tarefas                                                                                      | An document used to ensure that all tasks are completed to keep the aircraft safe and operational.                                                                                |
| **Completion Notes**               | Notas de Conclusão                                                                                    | An expression used to describe a group of, optional, observations registered after a maintenance.                                                                                 |
| **Fleet**                          | Frota                                                                                                 | A term used to characterize a group of aircrafts.                                                                                                                                 |
| **Flight Distance**                | Distância de voo                                                                                      | It is the distance of an flight in an route in kilometers.                                                                                                                        |
| **Flight Status**                  | Estado de um voo                                                                                      | It refers to the current status or condition of an flight.                                                                                                                        |
| **Flight Time**                    | Tempo de voo                                                                                          | It is an flight time of an aircraft in an route in hours.                                                                                                                         |
| **GeoJSON**                        | GeoJSON                                                                                               | Is an open standard format for representing simple geographical features and their non-spatial attributes, based on JSON.                                                         |
| **GMT**                            | Tempo Médio em Greenwich                                                                              | An acronyn for Greenwich Mean Time, it is a time zone observed in some European and African countries.                                                                            |
| **Graphical Representations**      | Representações Gráficas                                                                               | A diagram and charts that illustrate various aspects of an aircraft".                                                                                                             |
| **IATA**                           | Associação de Tranportes Aéreos Internacionais                                                        | Is an acronym for Internacional Air Transport Association.                                                                                                                        |
| **IATA Code**                      | Código IATA                                                                                           | Is a unique three-letter identifier assigned to airports and cities.                                                                                                              |
| **KML**                            | Linguagem de Keyhole Markup                                                                           | Acronym for Keyhole Markup Language, it's a XML notation for expressing geographic annotation and visualization within two-dimensional maps and three-dimensional Earth browsers. |
| **Maintenance Expected Duration**  | Duração Esperada de uma Manutenção                                                                    | Is the expected time duration for an maintenance in hours.                                                                                                                        |
| **Maintenance Alert**              | Alerta de Manutenção                                                                                  | A system notification triggered when an aircraft reaches specific thresholds, indicating it is due for scheduled maintenance.                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Maintenance Part**               | Peça de Manutenção                                                                                    | An expression used to specify a specific aircraft component that needs maintenance.                                                                                               |
| **Maintenance Record**             | Registo de Manutenção                                                                                 | Is a documented account of all maintenance activities performed on an asset, including inspections, repairs, and modifications.                                                   |
| **Maintenance Supervisor**         | Supervisor de Manutenção                                                                              | A professional who supervises a Maintenance Technician.                                                                                                                           |
| **Maintenance Technician**         | Técnico de Manutenção                                                                                 | A professional who inspects, repairs, and maintains aircraft to ensure they are safe and operational.                                                                             |
| **Manufacturer**                   | Fabricante                                                                                            | A company or entity that designs, builds, test, sells, and maintains aircraft or aircraft engines.                                                                                |
| **Minimum Aircraft Range**         | Alcance Mínimo de uma Aeronave                                                                        | Is is the minimum range in kilometers of an airraft.                                                                                                                              | 
| **Network**                        | Rede                                                                                                  | A term used to characterize a group of routes.                                                                                                                                    |
| **Route**                          | Rota                                                                                                  | A term used to specify a flight between two airport.                                                                                                                              |
| **Runway**                         | Pista                                                                                                 | A term used to characterize a track where aircrafts poise and takeoff.                                                                                                            |
| **Technical Diagram**              | Diagrama Técnico                                                                                      | A detailed representation that shows the various component and systems of an aircraft.                                                                                            |
| **Template**                       | Modelo                                                                                                | A structured tool used to ensure proper inspection, maintenance, overhaul or modification of aircraft systems.                                                                    |
| **Time Zone**                      | Fuso Horário                                                                                          | Is a region that observes the same standard time for legal, commercial, and social purposes.                                                                                      |
| **UTC**                            | Tempo Coordenado Universal                                                                            | An acronyn for Coordinated Universal Time, it is the primary time standard used globally to regulate clocks and time zones.                                                       |
| **Active Route**                   | Rota Ativa                                                                                            | A route that is available for operational use.                                                                                                                                    |
| **Departure Airport**              | Aeroporto de Origem                                                                                   | The registered airport from which a route starts.                                                                                                                                 |
| **Destination Airport**            | Aeroporto de Destino                                                                                  | The registered airport where a route ends.                                                                                                                                        |
| **Estimated Flight Time**          | Tempo Estimado de Voo                                                                                 | The expected duration of a route, represented in hours.                                                                                                                           |
| **Inactive Route**                 | Rota Inativa                                                                                          | A route that has been deactivated but remains stored in the system for historical and administrative purposes.                                                                    |
| **Minimum Aircraft Capacity**      | Capacidade Mínima de uma Aeronave                                                                     | The minimum number of seats or passengers required for an aircraft to operate a route.                                                                                            |
| **Route History**                  | Histórico de uma Rota                                                                                 | A record of relevant changes made to a route, such as creation, update or deactivation.                                                                                           |
| **Route ID**                       | Identificador de uma Rota                                                                             | A unique identifier for a route, usually based on the departure and destination IATA codes.                                                                                       |
| **Route Search**                   | Pesquisa de Rotas                                                                                     | A functionality that allows routes to be searched by origin airport, destination airport, or both.                                                                                |
